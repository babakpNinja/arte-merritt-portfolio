Working Thesis

From chatbots to agents

The 2016-2022 chatbot wave taught the industry how humans talk to software. The 2024+ agentic wave is teaching software how to talk to software. The unit of work is shifting from "answer a question" to "complete a task across systems."

Observability is the next picks-and-shovels

Every revolution needs an analytics layer. Dashbot was that for chatbots; Reconify is that for the LLM-application era. You cannot deploy what you cannot measure.

Distribution is the moat

Model labs are commoditizing. The companies that win are the ones with hyperscaler distribution (Microsoft, AWS, Google) and the partner ecosystem to actually deploy. That is the work.

AI employees, not AI tools

The end state is not "chatbot in the corner of the page." It is autonomous teammates with goals, memory, and the authority to act. Phantom, Devin, Operator — the shape of the next decade.
